About

This smart device application allows you to send a tip directly to your favourite performing queen, live and in real time! Simply scan the personal QR code of the performer and go to their tipping page. No more need to carry loose change or various low denomination bills. Tipping amount starts as low as 20 cents! And 100% of the tipped amount goes straight to the performer.
